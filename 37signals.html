 The basics are beautiful

 No hidden fees or secret prices
We’re famous for fast and friendly customer service. We work hard to make sure we live up to that reputation every day. See how our customers rate our service.  Clarity is king

 Software should be easy
Buzzwords, lingo, and sensationalized sales-and-marketing-speak have no place at 37signals. We communicate clearly and honestly. Our products are intuitive. You’ll pick them up in seconds or minutes, not hours, days or weeks. We don’t sell you training because you don’t need it.
